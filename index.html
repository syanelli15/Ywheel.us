Power & Specs

IndySpeedControl 75.6V (18S2P) pack + Thor301 VESC. ~28 mph top speed. Cannoncore V2 6” hub motor provides 4000W of power.

Future Plans

Prototype 1 underway; production model in concept. Focus: XR parts compatibility in a compact EUC.
